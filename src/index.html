About Our Academy

Welcome to Panamerican Taekwondo Academy

Located in the heart of Winston-Salem, NC, Panamerican Taekwondo Academy is dedicated to teaching traditional Taekwondo while fostering personal growth, discipline, and confidence. Our experienced instructors are committed to helping students of all ages and skill levels achieve their martial arts goals.

Whether you're looking to improve your fitness, learn self-defense, compete in tournaments, or simply find a supportive community, our academy provides a welcoming environment where everyone can thrive.
